  • Some of my artworks and projects ↓

  • Ghost Guy

    This ghost guy was made in blender with the Lightning Boy Shader add on for shading. He was designed and modeled by me. The mouth uses shape keys and a boolean to get interesting results fast. The rig was primaraly for learning shape keys and it turned out to be very succesful.

  • Kidd Character

    This Kidd character was designed for a game concept. She was modeled following good modeling techniques for animation. Current plans for her are to finish facial rigging, make some animations and possibly import her into a game engine.

  • Rubberhose Style Pig

    This is a model of a pig made in Blender inspired by the “rubber hose era” of animation. This model also uses the Lightning Boy Shader just like the Ghost Guy. I designed my models to be fully rigged even though they need some additional work to be exported out of Blender. The box at the side is a screenshot of the objects and some of the bones in the scene to show what it took to create the final product.
